how do you apply for a CTO position?

A wise man once told me: “if you want to say something, write it down”.

Here I am writing my thoughts about: How do you apply for CTO position?

The easy way is to through your network. Actually being found through your network and become the CTO of a great start-up. This is exactly how I’ve ended up at Peaks.

But here is the hard way: Applying for a CTO position to a company in a “blind date” style.

I came across a quite interesting opportunity for a position of CTO at GlobalInternet.

The big question is, can you create a vision and transmit it through the recruitment process?

Let’s see …

vision

A true internet provider will be invisible. To be successful you need to  unless:

  • Get internet access
  • No internet
  • Statistics on usage (only for business)

For no other reason I would even want to know how I get connected.

This leads to a vision defined around those three
